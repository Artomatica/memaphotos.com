Why Choose Mema?

Six reasons to switch from Google Photos

  • Privacy by Default

    All photo processing happens locally on your device. Your photos never leave your phone unless you choose to back them up.

  • Smarter Semantic Search

    Find photos using natural language. Search for "sunset beach" or "high-contrast blue lighting" and get accurate results instantly.

  • Battery Efficient

    Smart indexing only runs when charging. No surprise battery drain. Optimized for modern mobile processors.

  • No Data Mining

    We don't track you, analyze your photos for ads, or sell your data. Your memories are yours alone.

  • Bring Your Own Storage

    Use your own S3-compatible storage or keep everything local. No vendor lock-in. Full control over your data.

  • Deterministic Results

    No AI hallucinations or random summaries. Get the exact photos you're looking for, every time.
